STAKING

Locked Stake
Locked Staking allows users to lock DLA tokens and collect DLA tokens by adding liquidity to Pancakeswap and then locking their LP tokens for a specified period of time. The APR rate is much higher than the unlocked staking because your DLA tokens remain locked until the time you have chosen.
Start
Unlocked Stake
Unlocked Staking allows users to stake DLA tokens and collect DLA tokens by adding liquidity to Pancakeswap and then staking LP tokens. The APR rate is lower than the locked staking, but you can withdraw money at any time without paying commission fees.
