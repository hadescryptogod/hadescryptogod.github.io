DAO COMMUNITY SYSTEM

The incubator via on-chain community voting is only the first step in the journey ahead. The greatest benefit of the Diolaunch DAO system for the community is helping them launch products and services through feedback loops that lead to brand status successful projects that their future communities will love to be involved in and support. All projects wishing to be published on the Launchpad must undergo decentralized community approval. Approved projects are then published on the website through the decentralized system. All community members participating in the voting are rewarded with DLA so that they can invest in published projects more easily and with certain profits. In this way, both the published project and the voting user gain profit
