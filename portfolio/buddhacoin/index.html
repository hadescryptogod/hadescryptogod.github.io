WHAT IS
BUDDHACOIN

BuddhaCoin is a utility-based, charity token deployed on the Binance Smart Chain through the BSC Medium. BuddhaCoin's vision is to develop an sustainable ecosystem from a series of utilities, who's sole goal is to make the world a better place. BuddhaCoin's ecosystem will also generate revenue to be distributed to charities in a unique fashion.

WHAT CHARITY DO
WE SUPPORT

BuddhaCoin is a unique charity token because the project does not support a specific cause. Instead, BuddhaCoin reaches out to crypto influencers & celebrities (via Twitter) to have them decide the charity. All the celebrities have to do is reply to our comment, and BuddhaCoin will donate up to $1,000 to their chosen charity. BUT WAIT, THERE'S MORE! If the celebrity retweets the donation post, BuddhaCoin will donate up to $5,000 to the same charity! Think about the exposure of the good BuddhaCoin is doing, from a celebrity retweet!!

WHAT ARE THE
UTILITIES

BuddhaCoin's first utility is a telegram bot that can run a Buy Contest for any BSC or ETH group. Types of contests the bot can run are Big Buy Contests, Last Buy Contests, and Random Buy Contests. In addition, a community designed contest is coming! This utility will be available prior to launch and free to the public to use. As for BuddhaCoin's other utilities, they will be revealed at a later date, due to obvious chart management & marketing reasons.
